Annual Fall and Spring Research Forum

CURB is proud to host the 30th Annual Undergraduate Spring Research Forum. The Annual Undergraduate Spring Research Forum is the largest undergraduate research forum held at Cornell, with over a hundred undergraduates presenting in all fields of research (physics, philosophy, art history, biological engineering, etc.) and over 700 attendees.

Where: Physical Science Building Atrium
When: Wednesday, April 22nd, 2015 from 5:30PM to 7:00PM

If you have any questions or comments, please do not hesitate to email tmd222@cornell.edu
