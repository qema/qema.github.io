Me

Hello! I'm Andrew Wang. I'm a Dean's Scholar at Cornell majoring in Computer Science and Philosophy. I'm interested in social and information networks and the game-theoretic roots of social interaction — social processes are fascinatingly fundamental. Working with Professor Cristian Danescu-Niculescu-Mizil.
