Neon Birds

This is a simulation of bird flocking.

Birds follow three rules when flocking:

  1. Go in the same direction as other birds (adhesion)
  2. Stay close to other birds (cohesion)
  3. Don't bump into other birds (repulsion)

The birds are colored by the direction they're facing, to distinguish groups.

Play around with the parameters and control the red bird with your mouse. Happy flocking!
